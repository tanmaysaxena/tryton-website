Accounting Simplified


The simple user interface of the Accounting app allows you to maintain your company books and do all company/ business/ financial accounting at one place, quick and easy. You don’t need to be an accountant to use it. It’s that simple!

Reporting

It will simplify all your complex data and convert in into easy to understand graphs and reports. All reports related to Trial Balance, Trading A/c, Profit & Loss A/c, Balance Sheet, Debtor’s Balance, Creditors Balance, Cash/Bank Fund position and many more are covered in this module.


Accurate

Keep all your data and finance system in sync with the the rest of your company in real-time. Real Time Synchronization makes your workflow extremely convenient and speeds up the synchronization process considerably.

MultiPurpose

The financial module in Tryton provides financial functionality and analysis reports for different departments and cost centers consisting of a wide range of features from Financial Accounting to Enterprise controlling.


Manage all your accounts at one place!

This module will assist in recording day to day transactions like receiving payments from customers, payments to vendors by raising cheques or through cash and journal entries. Over and above financial module records the data during


  • Creation of a Customer Account
  • Creating of a Vendor Account
  • Creating a Bank Account
  • Creating a General Ledger Account
  • Enhancing Chart of Accounts

    • Or anything that is required to maintain customers information.


    Take Intelligent Decisions

    This module will take care of all accounts related entries and their impact on the whole system. How the finance comes and how it is been utilised, Total flow of money and total expenditures, everything will be shown here.
